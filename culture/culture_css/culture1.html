The Parada Sillimania was held on August 26, at 2:30 PM. My role there was a performer/dancer. The Cheercom was responsible for handling our practices we held for this event. This event was the parade for the celebration of Silliman Founders.

This was going to be my first time ever in a parade where I was part of the lead performance. I was also a new student to Silliman. This was literally going out of my comfort zone but still, I really enjoyed the parade. To forget my embarassment, I just had to focus on myself and on my batchmates that was with me. It was very enjoyable and very fun watching a senior don a horse mask over himself. The parade itself was less nerve wracking, than the whole dance show at the ball field. I did quite a number of mistakes as we had less time than other dancers, and I was also less skilled. But it was enjoyable at the end.

This event made me socialize more. Not just the event. Joining the committee that allowed me to perform was practically a leap of my own comfort zone. But I liked it. Given the chance next year, I would definitely perform again and this time do it without mistakes (hopefully).

Because of this activity, I was able to perform a dance routine. It also gives me a reason to go outside more other than staying in my house. The event and the committee was a definite help in increasing my social outlook in life.
