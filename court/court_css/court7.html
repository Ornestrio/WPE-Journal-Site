This was an event held in the Daro gym, in December 8. It was at 8 AM, in which I was a co-organizer. I brought snacks and some ice packs for the players who were all CCS students, in which some of them were my friends. This event was organized by the FBPC team.

In the event, I brought some ice cooler and packs of ice. I also used my water bottle as the ice cooler since the ice cooler itself didn’t seem like it was doing it’s job well. I didn’t drink from my water bottle but we used plastic cups to distribute water to the players. When the teams played against eachother, I made sure the water they would drink is cold, and watched them play the games. After it was almost finished, I made sure to clean any wrappers and cups left behind.

In this event, I would continue my involvement in the organization as a helper. I see myself as a helper to help achieve it’s goals. I would like to continue working with the group to further develop my skills working in an organization

Because of this activity, I learned how to manage an event. Although it was only to make sure the players are well refreshed, I am satisfied with my role. I should start from small roles, to see if I can handle big roles later on. This event helped me develop the feeling of responsibility.
